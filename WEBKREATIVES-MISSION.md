## Mission

WebKreatives builds custom websites for clients who want more than a template. The goal is to turn each client's imagination, budget, needs, and business direction into a tailored online presence that feels specific to them.

## Positioning

WebKreatives is not focused only on small businesses anymore. The brand should speak to clients who want a custom website, whether the request is simple, premium, unusual, experimental, or visually bold.

The promise is flexible execution: if a client can imagine it, WebKreatives will find a practical way to design and build it within the agreed budget and scope.

## Differentiators

- No generic templates as the core offer.
- Custom design shaped around the client's idea, brand, audience, budget, and needs.
- Open to unconventional and visually ambitious website concepts.
- Uses current AI-assisted workflows for design, content, development, iteration, and delivery.
- Better value than traditional agencies through faster iteration, flexible scope, and modern tooling.
- Can support the full website lifecycle: concept, design, build, hosting, SEO, support, maintenance, and improvement.
