About Lena
"Photography is the art of frozen time — the ability to store emotion and feelings within a frame."

Lena van der Berg

I am a portrait and brand photographer based in Utrecht, working with individuals, creatives, and businesses who want imagery that is honest, considered, and quietly beautiful.

With a background in fine art and over eight years behind the lens, my approach is unhurried: we talk, we set the scene, and we let the images come naturally. No forced smiles. No generic backdrops.

My studio is a calm, north-facing space in the heart of Utrecht — always available for natural-light sessions. Outdoor locations across Utrecht and surroundings by arrangement.
