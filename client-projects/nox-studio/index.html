How it is made

From raw metal
to finished piece

01

Sketch & Design

Every piece starts as a pencil sketch. I draw until the shape feels right — sometimes that takes an hour, sometimes a week.

02

Cut & Shape

Sheet metal is cut with a jeweller's saw, then shaped by hand with a hammer, mandrel, and patience on my workbench at home.

03

Solder & Refine

Joints are soldered with a butane torch, then filed smooth. The piece is pickled, rinsed, and shaped again until it is perfect.

04

Polish & Send

Each piece is hand-polished to a mirror finish, wrapped with care, and sent directly from my home to yours — no middleman, ever.
