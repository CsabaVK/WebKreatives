Our story

A sanctuary
for your skin

Luna Beauty Studio was born from a simple belief: every person deserves to feel completely at home in their own skin. Nestled in Rotterdam, we offer a calm, unhurried space where professional skincare meets genuine human warmth.

Our treatments are built on clean, science-backed ingredients and personalised care. Whether you're seeking a deep facial, a relaxing massage, or expert brow styling, you'll always leave feeling like yourself — only better.
