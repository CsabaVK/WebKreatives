Project flow
From start to launch

How the project works

This is our standard workflow. Short, practical and without agency jargon.

1

Onboarding & confirmation

We confirm the chosen package, the contact person, the timeline and the desired direction of the site.

2

Client delivery

You provide the logo, preferred colours, texts, photos, contact details and any additional input.

3

Design & build

We translate your input into a professional, mobile-friendly and clear website within the agreed scope.

4

Feedback & revisions

You receive a preview and ideally send your feedback all at once, so we can work through it efficiently.

5

Final check

We review content, mobile layout, forms, links and basic SEO before the site goes live.

6

Launch

After approval we take the site live. Optional maintenance or support can begin afterwards.

Important:

Delays in content, feedback or access automatically push back the timeline. That's normal for website projects, so fast delivery really makes a difference here.

What we need from you

  • Logo or provisional brand mark
  • Preferred colours or style direction
  • Texts or raw content
  • Photos of your company, team or work
  • Contact details + opening hours
  • Any domain/hosting/login credentials

What you can expect from us

  • Fast and clear communication
  • Professional visual direction
  • Mobile-friendly build
  • Clear feedback moments
  • Website within agreed package boundaries
  • Clean launch without unnecessary hassle

Client questions & intake

This page is intentionally practical. No unnecessary questions — only what's needed to build well and fast.

1. Do you already have a logo?
Preferably send PNG, SVG or PDF.
2. Do you have preferred colours or an existing brand identity?
E.g. black/white, luxury, modern, warm, clean, minimalist.
3. What look and feel should the site have?
E.g. professional, premium, friendly, local, modern, simple.
4. What contact details should appear on the website?
5. Which services or products should be clearly visible?
6. Do you already have texts, or should we work from raw input?
7. Do you have your own photos that we should use?
8. Is there anything that absolutely must or must not appear on the site?

Revisions, agreements & terms

This page is intended as a short, client-friendly summary of how we work together. Not a heavy legal document, but clear enough to set expectations properly.

Revision rounds

  • Starter: 2 revision rounds
  • Business: 3 revision rounds
  • Growth: 5 revision rounds
  • Please bundle feedback as much as possible per round

Timeline

  • Starter: delivered in 5 days
  • Business: delivered in 1 week
  • Growth: delivered in 2–3 weeks
  • Only achievable with timely input and feedback

Key working agreements

  • The website is built within the boundaries of the chosen package.
  • Extra pages, extra features or new requests outside the original scope are additional work and will always be discussed first.
  • The client is responsible for the timely delivery of texts, photos, business information and required access.
  • Delays in feedback or content automatically push back the agreed delivery date.
  • After launch, the client can opt for a monthly plan covering hosting, backups, security and further support.
  • The site ultimately belongs to the client as per the agreed delivery and setup.
