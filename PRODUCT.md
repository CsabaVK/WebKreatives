# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Dutch small business owners (SMEs, local entrepreneurs, service providers) who feel invisible online and want a professional website without agency prices or months of waiting. They use Google to find web designers, compare prices, and make decisions based on social proof and portfolio quality. They are busy, skeptical of hidden costs, and respond to directness.

## Product Purpose

WebKreatives converts website visitors into paying clients for custom web design services. Pricing starts at €199, delivery in 1–2 weeks. Success means a visitor fills in the contact form or calls. The brand competes against expensive agencies and cheap templates by being fast, personal, transparent, and Dutch.

## Brand Personality

Direct. Crafted. Confident.

Voice is no-nonsense Dutch entrepreneur energy — honest about pricing, proud of quality, zero corporate jargon. The brand should feel like it was made by someone who genuinely cares, not a faceless agency.

## Anti-references

- Generic Wix/Squarespace template look — centered stacks, cookie-cutter stock photography
- Cheap freelancer aesthetics — too many fonts/colors, banner-ad buttons, crowded layouts
- Hipster minimalism — too sparse to communicate value, white space as an excuse for emptiness
- Big-agency glassmorphism — dark floating cards, excessive gradients, metric-stat heroes

## Design Principles

1. **Size signals confidence.** Large, unapologetic typography. The headline should feel like a billboard, not a brochure.
2. **Show the work first.** Portfolio quality is the strongest proof. Don't bury it.
3. **Earn every fold.** Each scroll section must have a single clear job. No filler sections.
4. **Dutch directness in copy.** No fluff intros. Lead with the strongest statement. Cut everything that restates what the previous line said.
5. **Motion as craft, not decoration.** Animations reveal intent — scroll choreography should feel like turning pages in a well-designed book, not a theme-park ride.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — all GSAP animations must check this. Dutch as primary language, English toggle preserved.
